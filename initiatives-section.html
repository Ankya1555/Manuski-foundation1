Other Activities

◆ COVID-19 Support Initiative

During the COVID-19 pandemic, our team at Manusaki Foundation took proactive steps to help the underprivileged. We provided free meals, medical kits, oxygen cylinders, and transportation for those in need. All efforts were funded through personal contributions, ensuring maximum impact without external aid.

◆ Food & Cloth Donation Drive

On 9th October 2021, Manusaki Foundation launched a donation campaign, distributing 500+ blankets and warm clothes to homeless individuals. The foundation also arranged meal distribution programs, ensuring no one had to sleep hungry.

◆ Manusaki Foundation: Community Welfare Program

Alongside environmental efforts, Manusaki Foundation has launched multiple community programs, including food distribution, health awareness campaigns, and educational support for underprivileged children. These initiatives aim to create long-lasting positive change in society.

◆ ◆ 2019 Flood Relief

During the devastating floods of 2019, Manuski Foundation played a crucial role in providing food, medical aid, and shelter to affected families. Our volunteers worked tirelessly to distribute relief materials and support the rehabilitation efforts.

◆ COVID-19 Relief Drive

In response to the COVID-19 crisis, Manuski Foundation provided essential ration kits, medical supplies, and financial aid to struggling families. We also ensured that frontline workers had the necessary protective gear during the pandemic.

◆ Bird Feeding & Environmental Care<

To protect nature and support biodiversity, Manuski Foundation installed food and water stations for birds, ensuring they have access to nourishment during harsh seasons. We also initiated tree plantation drives to promote environmental conservation.
