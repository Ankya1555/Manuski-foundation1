About Us

The Manuski Foundation, Maharashtra, is a registered Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) based in Sangli. Since 2017, we have been committed to helping underprivileged communities and supporting various humanitarian initiatives to create a positive impact on society.


Waste management plays a crucial role in reducing environmental pollution and promoting sustainability. Our foundation actively works towards waste reduction, encouraging the recycling and reuse of materials such as paper, cans, and glass. We also focus on the safe disposal of solid, liquid, and hazardous waste to protect public health and the environment. substances.


Our organization is dedicated to spreading awareness about the harmful effects of improper plastic disposal and other waste on our surroundings. We educate communities on responsible waste management and sustainable practices to preserve nature for future generations. "Helping Hands is Our Motto."

What we Do

COVID-19 Relief Work

During the COVID-19 pandemic, we provided food, medicines, and daily essentials to families struggling to survive. Our team personally funded and distributed meals to those in need, ensuring that no one suffered from hunger.

Animal & Bird Care Initiative

We installed food and water stations for birds and animals, ensuring they had access to nourishment, especially during extreme weather conditions.

Blood Donation Camps

We organize regular blood donation camps to ensure a steady supply of life-saving blood for hospitals and patients in critical need.

Food & Cloth Donations

We distribute food, clothing, and essential items to underprivileged families, ensuring that no one is left behind in times of hardship.
